The City

Rio de Janeiro is a major Brazilian seaside city, famous for its Copacabana and Ipanema beaches, the 38-meter-tall Christ the Redeemer statue atop Corcovado, and Sugarloaf Mountain, a granite peak with cable cars leading to its summit. The city is also known for its large favelas. The thrilling Carnival, with its parade floats, extravagant costumes, and samba dancers, is considered the biggest in the world.
